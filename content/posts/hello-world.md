## Why I started this blog

TruePositive is where I document my security journey — CTF writeups, research notes, techniques I've learned, and tools I've built.

The name comes from the concept in detection engineering: a **true positive** is when an alert fires and it's actually malicious. No noise. Just signal.

That's the goal here — real findings, real techniques, no fluff.

## What to expect

- **CTF writeups** — HackTheBox, TryHackMe, real competitions
- **Web security** — vulnerabilities, bypasses, exploitation chains
- **Red team** — tradecraft, tooling, OPSEC
- **Malware analysis** — reversing, behavior analysis, detection

Stay tuned.
